How It Works

Our system leverages advanced algorithms and real-time data to provide optimized bus schedules and routes. Here's a quick overview:

  • Data Collection: Real-time data from various sources like traffic updates, passenger demand, and weather conditions.
  • Schedule Optimization: The system automatically generates schedules based on the collected data, ensuring timely and efficient bus services.
  • Route Management: Dynamic route planning adjusts routes in real-time to avoid delays and minimize travel time.
  • User Interaction: Passengers can view updated schedules, check the status of their bus, and plan their journey seamlessly.
